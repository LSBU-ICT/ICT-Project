Complaint Letters

Please see below template letters to complain to a trader. It should help the trader to gain understand on your complaint, your rights and their legal obligation to resolve the problem.

The letters can be sent by post or email. If you feel more comfortable speaking, you can tell the trader the content within the template letters.

Before using the letters, you can see your consumer rights in [ FAQ ]

How to use the letters:

1) Click on the "Full Screen" button on the bottom right corner of the letter you would like to use.
2) The letter can be open and modify on one drive (if account available).
3) Alternatively, you can download and modify on your computer.
4) Sent the letter to the seller by post or email.
